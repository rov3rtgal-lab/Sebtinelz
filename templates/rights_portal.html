Philippine Law

Applicable Legal Protections

RA 11313 · Workplace

Safe Spaces Act

Mandates zero-tolerance policies against gender-based harassment in all workplaces, including OJT sites, schools, and online environments.

  • CODI Required: All workplaces must maintain a Committee on Decorum and Investigation.
  • 10-Day Rule: Management must respond to complaints within 10 days.
  • Whistleblower Shield: You cannot be penalized for reporting harassment.
  • Liability Extends: Employers are accountable even for third-party harassment.
  • Victim Support: Psychological assistance and counseling must be provided.
RA 10524 · Employment

PWD Employment Act

Guarantees equal employment opportunities for Persons with Disabilities in both public and private sectors. Discrimination is a punishable offense.

  • 1% Reservation: Companies with 100+ employees must reserve at least 1% of positions for PWDs.
  • Equal Pay: PWDs are entitled to the same wages and benefits as other employees.
  • Tax Incentives: Employers receive deductions for hiring and accommodating PWDs.
  • No Discrimination: Refusing employment solely due to disability is unlawful.
  • Accessibility: Workplaces must provide reasonable accommodations.
DOLE Order · OJT

Student Intern Rights

DOLE guidelines protect student trainees from labor exploitation and ensure safe, fair, and educationally meaningful OJT programs.

  • 8-Hour Cap: OJT students may not be required to render more than 8 hours per day.
  • Hazard-Free: Interns must not be assigned to hazardous or heavy physical labor.
  • Safety Insurance: Mandatory insurance coverage for OJT-related injuries.
  • School Duty of Care: Institutions bear responsibility for off-campus incidents.
  • MOA Required: A formal agreement must exist between school and company.
RA 10022 · Overseas

Overseas Worker Safety Rights

The Migrant Workers and Overseas Filipinos Act protects OFWs from abuse, illegal recruitment, and passport confiscation abroad.

  • Passport Retention: Employers abroad cannot legally hold your passport.
  • Embassy Access: You have the right to contact the Philippine Embassy or POLO 24/7.
  • POEA-Verified Only: Only agencies accredited by POEA may deploy workers legally.
  • Contract Compliance: Your employment contract governs your terms — any deviation is actionable.
RA 11313 · Education

Academic & Training Institutions

Schools and training centers are legally required to maintain safe environments for all students, including off-campus OJT placements.

  • Off-Campus Liability: Protection applies during internship hours even at a company site.
  • Peer Harassment: Student-on-student harassment is equally covered by the law.
  • Admin & Criminal Action: Victims may pursue administrative, civil, or criminal cases.
  • Remedial Support: Schools must provide access to counseling services.
